Biography of Mike Tyson

Mike Tysons' Career

Mike Tyson, one of the greatest heavyweight boxers of his time. WIth his record being 50 wins, 6 losses, he has had a very successful carrer. Mike had his first fight March 6, 1985. Mike went on to become a very respected boxer and man. With him still being relevant, attending podcasts, and occasional tv apperances.

Mikes Childhood

Mike was born in Fort Greene,Brooklyn,New York City on June 30,1966. His dad left his life before Mike even turned two years old. This led him to the street life which caused trips in and out of the juvenile system. One of Tysons most popular childhood story is about his bond with pigeons. As a young kid, Tyson kept pigeons. A bully killed one of Tysons pigeon causing him to punch his bully, with Tyson claiming" That was the first time I threw a punch."

Beggining of Career

Mike Tysons' first proffesional fight took place on March 6,1985, with Mike fighting at just 18 years old. Within his first year as a boxer he had 15 fights winning them all, with majority being won by KO. With this he got his nickname "Iron Mike". He has said he got think nickname from his friend Carlos Santos.

Later in carrer

Later in his carreer, Mike was a very well respected fighter. With many wins for titleships, holding three at once, he was a very big deal. Following a divorce, there were allegations that he had physically abused his ex wife. With this, not too long later, he had lost a fight for the championship. With this upsetting loss, he went on to win his next four fights. Again, he was hit with another allegation, this one being a rape charge. This one got him sent to prison for a little less than three years. After release, Mike had what is to be considered one of his most memorable moment. His fight against Evander Holyfield. This fight he was disqualified for biting of the opponents ear!
